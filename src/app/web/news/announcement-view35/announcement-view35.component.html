2023 KSEA Election Results Announcement

April 8, 2023

The Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA) is pleased to announce the results of its 2023 general election. The ballot counting took place at the Election Committee and KSEA HQ meeting on April 8, 2023. All ballots have been counted according to the rules that were pre-established by the Election Committee, and no election-related irregularities were found.

Voting Results:

  • Number of Eligible Voters: 3356
  • Number of Valid Ballots Received: 567 (16.90% of eligible voters; 10% quorum was met)
