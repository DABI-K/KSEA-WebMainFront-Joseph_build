Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

2023 KSEA Election Announcement

January 17, 2023

The 2023 KSEA General Election to elect President-Elect, Vice Presidents, Auditor, and Technical Group Councilors will take place through the secured e-voting system during the online voting period of March 15 to April 7, 2023.

The following officer positions will be filled in this year’s election:

  • President-Elect
  • Vice Presidents 1 and 2
  • Auditor
  • Councilors for Technical Group A-1 (Physics), Technical Group A-2 (Chemistry), Technical Group A-3 (Mathematics, Statistics, Geo/Atmospheric/Ocean Sciences), Technical Group B-1 (Health Sciences: Medical Science, Veterinary Medicine, Pharmaceutical Science, Public Health, Nursing, etc.), Technical Group B-2 (Agricultural Sciences: Agriculture, Ecology, Food, Nutrition, etc.), Technical Group C-2 (Chemical, Textile, Energy, and Nuclear Engineering), and Technical Group C-3 (Mechanical, Aerospace, and Naval Engineering); and a YG Representative

Voting Method:Electronic voting via the Internet (e-voting)
This year’s voting will be done by electronic means only. e-Voting announcement will be sent via e-mail on or after Wednesday, March 15, 2023.

Voting Period: Wednesday, March 15, 2023 – Friday, April 7, 2023
KSEA Election Site will accept the e-Voting ballots during the voting period specified above. Any ballot cast after April 7, 11:59 PM (EDT) will be considered ineligible.

Voter Eligibility:
Any member who paid his or her dues at least once in the last two terms from May 1, 2021 to February 28, 2023 will be given an election ballot if a valid email address is provided and recorded in the membership system. Any member without a valid email address will not be given the election ballot according to the Election Policy.

Voting Procedure:
The election information will be sent via e-mail on or after March 15, 2023. An eligible voter will need to log in to the KSEA Election Site with his/her KSEA ID and password. Valid email address and information is required for the voter verification. Invalid inputs such as “.” and “1” will make the vote invalid. The election result will be announced on the website as soon as possible.

Potential Prizes to Yourself and Your Chapters:

  • Six different chapters with at least 25 eligible votes (total $3,000 support):
    (a) $750 to the chapter with the highest voting RATE, $500 to the 2nd & $250 to the 3rd; (b) $750 to the chapter with the highest NUMBER of votes, $500 to the 2nd highest & $250 to the 3rd. A chapter can take only one prize from the voting rate or number.
  • $50 Amazon gift card to 20 voters (Just by casting a vote, you will be entered a raffle)

The election result will be announced on the website on or after Saturday, April 8, 2023.
