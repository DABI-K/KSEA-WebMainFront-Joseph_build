Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Celebrating the life and career of KSEA 30th President Nak Ho Sung (Zoom: 7pm ET, Saturday, March 4)

March 29, 2023

Dear KSEA Members, families and friends,

I am writing to invite you to the online event at 7pm ET on Saturday, March 4th to celebrate the life and career of KSEA 30th President Nak Ho Sung who passed away in October 22, 2022 in Korea.

The KSEA community would like to extend our deepest condolences to Prof. Sungâ€™s family one more time.

Professor Sung was Professor of Chemical and Biological Engineering at Tufts University, was the Director and Founder of the Laboratory for Materials and Interfaces, and chaired the department of Chemical and Biological Engineering. Prior to joining Tufts University in 1978, he was a Research Associate and Assistant Professor at M.I.T. Professor Sung's research was in Polymer Science and Engineering, focused on structural composites and multiphase systems. He was an elected member of National Academy of Engineering of Korea.

The 30th President of the KSEA Prof. Sung had served on many KSEA Committees and contributed to numerous programs of KSEA. In addition, he was the President of the Korean American University Professor's Association in America (KAUPA), and had been a Technical Advisor to SK Group in Korea for 24 years.

I hope to see many of you and your families and friends at this event on March 4th. Korean language will be used during the entire zoom event.
