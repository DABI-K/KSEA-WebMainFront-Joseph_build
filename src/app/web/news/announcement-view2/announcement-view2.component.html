Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

The 3rd KSEA Distinguished Guest Series on January 11,
2023 with John S. Kim, the Co-Founder and CEO of SendBird

December 19, 2022

Dear KSEA members,

Happy holidays!!
I am writing to invite you to the 3rd KSEA Distinguished Guest Series on Wednesday, January 11, 2023 with John S. Kim, the Co-Founder and CEO of SendBird and the recipient of the 2022 KSEA Entrepreneurship of the Year Award.
SendBird is the no. 1 conversations platform for mobile apps, serving over 300M monthly chat users across the world’s leading companies such as DoorDash, Reddit, Yahoo!, Rakuten, Paytm, and Walgreens. Under John’s leadership, SendBird has raised $220M+ and officially became a unicorn in 2021. In this session, John Kim will speak about his journey to becoming CEO of SendBird. He will participate in a question-and-answer session following his talk.
I hope to see many of you and your families and friends at this meeting. The zoom link will be sent to KSEA members who registered (see below). I would greatly appreciate it if you could reach out to non-KSEA members to join or renew KSEA membership and attend this event.
Event Details:
Date: Wednesday, January 11th, 2023 at 5:30-6:30pm (PT), 8:30-9:30pm (ET)
Venue: Virtual. A zoom link will be provided on Monday, January 9th, 2023 to KSEA members who register by filling out this Google form (HERE)
Facilitator: Young-Kee Kim, Prof. of Physics at Univ. of Chicago, President of KSEA
Registration Deadline: Sunday, January 8, 2023
With warmest regards
Young-Kee Kim,
President of KSEA
