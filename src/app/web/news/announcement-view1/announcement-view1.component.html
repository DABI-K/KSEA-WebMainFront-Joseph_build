Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Shutdown of KSEA website 1/4~1/6/2023

January 3, 2023

Dear KSEA members,

Summary:

1. Shutdown of KSEA website 1/4~1/6/2023
2. New website opens on 1/7/2023
3. Password reset encouraged after 1/7/2023

We, the KSEA IT team, are excited to announce the launch of our brand-new website! This site has been designed to provide our members with a more intuitive and user-friendly experience.
Our new website features a modern design, improved navigation, and a host of new features, including an administrative function and a customer loyalty program such as lifetime membership. We have also revised a blog section where we will be sharing industry job openings, news, and updates on our website.
Please note that the old KSEA website will be shut down on January 4th, 2023, and the new website will open on January 7th, 2023. To ensure a seamless transition, we strongly encourage you to reset your password when the new website opens.
We are still working on other functionalities, so please be patient with us.
We hope you will take a moment to visit our new website and explore all that it has to offer.
Thank you for your continued support.
Sincerely,
KSEA IT team
