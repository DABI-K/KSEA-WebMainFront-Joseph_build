Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

StartUp Pitch Competition at UKC 2023

June 13, 2023

Dear KSEA members and friends,

KSEA is hosting its 5th Startup Pitch Competition (SPC) during the US-Korea Conference on August 4 and 5, 2023 at the Hyatt Regency DFW, Dallas TX, where about 30 invited startups in each of two categories (Idea Category and Startup Category) will go head-to-head to compete for various awards, including cash awards. Additional details, including eligibility requirements and timeline, can be found at the UKC SPC Website and the competition flyer attached. Your pitching may be given in English or Korean.

Please apply at https://bit.ly/KSEA2023SPC by Friday, June 30, 2023, 11:59 EDT. Questions? Send an email at spc.ksea@gmail.com.

You are also encouraged to attend the Innovation and Entrepreneurship Symposium (IES) during the UKC 2023. We invite all entrepreneurial-minded professionals and students to attend the IES during UKC 2023 and learn about the continually growing cross-border partnership opportunities in the US and Korea.

Thank you.
