Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Euna Yoon, the KSEA Administrative Manger received the MPS Award

December 26, 2023

Ms. Eun Yoon, the Administrative Manager at the KSEA HQ received the Outstanding Contribution Award by the Ministry of Personnel Management (MPM) of the Republic of Korea. This award is to be given to a working staff annually who contributed to the global collaborations and global HR program which aims to recruit talented Koreans who are working abroad.

Manager Yoon contributed to arrange the MOU with the Ministry of Personnel Management and KSEA in May 2023. The MOU signing ceremony with MPM was held at the headquarters office of KSEA in Vienna, Virginia on May 17, 2023. At the MOU ceremony, Seung-ho Kim, the Ministry of Personnel Management attended and signed the MOU with Young-Kee Kim, the 51st President of KSEA and Presidents and leaders of the 7 KSEA Affiliated Professional Societies (APS) including KLAM, KASBP, KITEE, KSCEE, NIH-KSA, KAPAL and KOTAA separately.

As a follow up process after the MOU, Euna Yoon closely worked with the MPM to proceed with the National Human Resources Database (NHRDB) projects towards KSEA members. The DB Application Form and official guidelines by MPM were circulated to KSEA members to collect personal information, register it in the National Human Resources Database, and obtain applicantsâ€™ consent for collection and use of such information. As a result of the active promotion, 150 KSEA members registered in NHRDB in the 1st round of the application in 2023. Euna Yoon arranged the follow up meeting with the KSEA Leadership and Minister of the Personal Management in Seoul on July 6, 2023, during the 1st World Congress of Korean Scientists and Engineers for the continuous collaborations with MPM and KSEA.

Euna Yoon has been working at the KSEA HQ since 2010, and her main areas of focus are to coordinate the R&D collaboration projects and events, fundraising and various businesses for the benefit of KSEA members.
