Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

STEP-UP 2023 Announcement

April 18, 2023

The 4th Science and Technology Entrepreneurship Partners’ Upscale Program (STEP-UP 2023) will take place in Newport Beach, CA from May 20 to 21, 2023!

Join us at STEP-UP 2023, where startups of every stage discover their full potential to become a unicorn. Our special focus this year is the intersection of “the Arts, Technology, and Entrepreneurship," and we're excited to offer a range of dynamic programs designed to help startups achieve success.

We've brought together a diverse range of experts for STEP-UP 2023. From VCs based in Silicon Valley to successful entrepreneurs and insightful consultants, our lineup is designed to provide valuable insights and possible funding opportunities for startups at every stage. Participating startups will have the chance to introduce their products and services through Elevator Pitch and Startup Pitch sessions.

Our Showcase of Success sessions will feature unicorn startups sharing their insider knowledge and know-hows of success. For those looking to take their startup to the next level, our Road to Success Sessions will cover critical topics such as marketing, business law, and funding. Whether you're a new entrepreneur or a seasoned founder, we have something for you at STEP-UP 2023. Don't miss out on this opportunity to learn, network, and grow your startup!
