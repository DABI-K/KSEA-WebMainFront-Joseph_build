Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

2024 KSEA Honors Announcement

January 24, 2024

The Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA) is pleased to accept nominations for 2024 Distinguished Service Membership (DSM) and Honorary Membership (HM) to recognize those who have contributed exemplarily to KSEA with distinction.

Eligibility

  • Distinguished Service Membership (DSM): KSEA members who have served KSEA with distinction.
  • Honorary Membership (HM): KSEA members at least 65 years old who have retired with a distinguished career.

Nomination Deadline: March 10, 2024

Nomination
Fill out the nomination form together with the KSEA Service-Point Table and submit the form to sejong@ksea.org. To be considered favorably, the total service points earned by a nominee should be greater than 125 for DSM, and the sum of his/her age and total service points should be greater than 100 for HM.

Evaluation Procedure
Honors & Awards Committee (HAC) will evaluate the nominated candidates and recommend the finalists to the Executive Committee (EC). The EC will approve the recommended HM. The EC will confirm the recommended DSMs and present them to the KSEA Council for approval. The finalists will be notified via email by the end of May 2024.

Recognition
The DSM, Distinguished Service Membership Award, and HM awardees will receive KSEA plaques while additional KSEA Gold Medals will be bestowed on the DSM awardees. Membership dues are waived for Distinguished Service and Honorary members. It is noted that there is no voting right for non-paid members.
