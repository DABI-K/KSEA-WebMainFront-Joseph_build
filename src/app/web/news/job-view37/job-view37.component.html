Job Description

Position Description

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA) is seeking a creative Operation Support Staff. If you are seeking an opportunity of playing a significant role in helping a non-profit organization while also earning income and enhancing your resume, this is the job for you.

The KSEA Operation Assistant Staff reports to Admin Directors. KSEA Operation Support Staff is accountable for a quality user support experience by providing visionary technical support and information systems management to ensure the success of the KSEA operations and events management. The KSEA Operation Support Staff assists IT staff to manage KSEA websites and information systems for the businesses of its head-quarter and local chapters/branches. This position is a 3-month contract with the potential to convert full-time (or part-time) employees under annual renewal.


About the Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

KSEA was established in 1971 as a non-profit professional organization and has grown to over 8,000 registered members with ~ 70 Local Chapter and Branches, ~30 Affiliated Professional Societies, and 13 Technical Groups across the United States. The organization's objectives are to promote the application of science and technology for the general welfare of society, foster international cooperation, especially between the U.S. and Korea, and help Korean-American Scientists and Engineers develop their full career potential. Details about KSEA are available from the university's website at https://www.ksea.org/.


Essential Duties and Responsibilities are listed below. Other duties may be assigned.

  1. Respond to user issues, answering inquiries and resolving more complicated issues firsthand.
  2. Collaborate with other HQ staffs/Committee members/members for larger projects and announcements, gaining a broad understanding of system through technical manuals.
  3. Answering questions, concerns, and complaints regarding membership.
  4. Providing information on membership fees, policies, processes, benefits, products, and services.
  5. Assessing member eligibility and processing payments, applications, and requests.

  7. Documenting all inquiries and maintaining accurate membership records.
  8. Support all demands of KSEA HQ Staff, Executive Committee, Directors Committee, and Local Chapters.
  9. Applies knowledge of computer software and hardware to assist users in resolving problems.
  10. Ability to prioritize effectively and efficiently meet deadlines and produce work in a timely manner.
  11. Must be effective in handling problems in the workplace, including anticipating, preventing, identifying, and solving problems, as necessary.
  12. Undertake other duties or special projects as assigned or emergency tasks.
  13. Performs other related duties as assigned.
