Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Your gift makes a difference for KSEA

June 14, 2023

Dear Members and Friends of KSEA,

Thank you for your support of the KSEA. We are very pleased to announce that we launched the official KSEA donation website at https://www.ksea.org/donation.

No matter the amount, your gift makes a difference. It helps us to further our mission and expand our programs. You have options to donate for specific KSEA activities including chapter support, education, innovation and entrepreneurship programs, and programs for next generations, mid-career members, and senior members.

We are deeply grateful for your generosity. Please also convey this message to your friends and colleagues. The donors do not have to be members of KSEA. Once again, thank you for your support.

Young-Kee Kim
President of KSEA
