Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Calling for Korean Parents and their TEENS in the U.S for the Volunteer Opportunities

March 15, 2024

Dear KSEA members,

NIH-KWiSE is pleased to announce the KSEA families (anywhere in the U.S.) with teens ages 12-17 to participate in our study called the Project ARISE (Asian Americans' Resilience, Identity, and Socialization of Engagement) by Dr. Cixin Wang and colleagues at University of Maryland.
Parents condition: Either one or both parents must be Korean, while the other parent must be of Asian descent (any).
Study outcome:
Ultimately, this study to inform ways to promote resilience and healthy development in Asian American families and guide prevention and intervention plans.
Their work has influenced important public policies for Asian Americans, especially in the domains of discrimination and anti-Asian hate.
If you have questions, please contact Dr. Cixin Wang (cxwang@umd.edu) directly.
Please consider participating as Korean American, with teens (ages 12-17), or forward this message to others who may be interested.
Your contribution to this survey will benefit our community and our children.

Thanks for your consideration.
NIH-KWiSE Steering Committee
