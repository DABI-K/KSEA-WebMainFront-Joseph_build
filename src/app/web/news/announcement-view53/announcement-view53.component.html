Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Inauguration message from the 52nd KSEA President

July 12, 2023

Dear members of KSEA:

It is with great pride and honor that I send this greeting to you as the newly inaugurated President of the KSEA. I extend my heartfelt gratitude to each and every one of you for entrusting me with this esteemed position, and to my utmost capacity, I am committed to serving the KSEA and its noble mission to advance scientific excellence and technological innovation.

I would like to take a moment to reflect on the remarkable journey that has led KSEA to this point. KSEA for the past 51 years has been a source of immense talent, ingenuity, and resilience. Our members have made significant contributions to various fields of science and engineering, leaving an indelible mark through their groundbreaking discoveries, inventions and entrepreneurship. The outstanding contributions of KSEA members were vividly showcased at the 1st World Congress of Korean Scientists and Engineers, held in Seoul, Korea from July 4th to July 7th, 2023.

As the president of KSEA, I had the honor of receiving honors on behalf of our organization (you can watch the video of the event here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8vtYGVdmziQ). However, this humbling experience has deeply reminded and moved me, underscoring the significant role played by those, who came before me, through their unwavering passion, elevated our organization to such esteemed heights.

Today, we find ourselves in an era of unprecedented opportunities and challenges. We face complex issues that demand our collective expertise, dedication, and collaboration. As the President of KSEA, I pledge to foster an inclusive and empowering environment where our members can leverage their diverse perspectives and experiences to address these challenges head-on.

KSEA will continue to excel as a catalyst for fostering meaningful network and collaborations for scientists and engineers in the US and Korea. KSEA will continue to be the platform for knowledge-sharing, mentorship, and professional development, empowering the next generation of innovators to realize their full potential. By cultivating a vibrant network of support, we can inspire one another, nurture our talents, and create a lasting impact for our world.

I am deeply committed to strengthening partnerships and collaborations with Korean organizations, institutions, and industry leaders. By forging strategic alliances, we can amplify our collective voice, advocate for policies that promote scientific advancement, and create opportunities for cross-disciplinary collaboration that drive transformative impacts.

I am truly humbled to lead such an exceptional association of brilliant minds, dedicated professionals, and passionate volunteers. Together, let us build upon the strong foundation laid by our predecessors and strive for even greater heights. By embracing diversity, fostering innovation, and nurturing the spirit of exploration, KSEA will advance the frontiers of science and engineering, and inspire future generations to dream big and change the world.

Thank you for your unwavering support, and I look forward to a productive and impactful tenure as the President of KSEA.

Yours sincerely,


Yongho Sohn
52nd President of KSEA
