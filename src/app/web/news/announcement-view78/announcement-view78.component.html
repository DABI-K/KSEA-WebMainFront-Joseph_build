Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Renew your KSEA membership by March 4, 2024 to vote for the 2024 KSEA Election

January 31, 2024

Dear KSEA members,


The 2024 KSEA General Election to elect President-Elect, Vice Presidents, Auditor, and Technical Group Councilors will take place using the secured e-voting system during the voting period from Monday, March 18 to Friday, April 12, 2024.

Any member who paid his or her dues at least once in the last two terms from May 1, 2022 to March 4, 2024 will be given an election ballot if a valid email address is provided and recorded in the membership system. Any member without a valid email address will not be given the election ballot according to the new Election Policy adopted by the Council. Please include your mobile phone number in your membership DB. Your mobile phone number will be used for verification purposes.
