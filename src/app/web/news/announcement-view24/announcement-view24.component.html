Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

The 2023 IDRONE KSEA Online CAMP is now scheduled for May 4 – 6, 2023

March 8, 2023

Dear KSEA members and families,

We are pleased to announce that the 2023 iDrone KSEA online camp is now scheduled for May 4 – 6, 2023 (See the attached flyer). This event is 100% ONLINE so we will ship the idrone development kit to individual homes in 2 weeks in advance. You can keep the drone after the camp. Please feel free to pass this information to your friends who might be interested in this event. Don’t forget to use a discount CODE from your local chapter after renewing your KSEA membership at https://ksea.org/signin.

A step-by-step registration instruction is also attached in this email. Basically, each student will walk through online learning modules, including drone building, coding, and mission planning independently during the iDrone event. If your children and their friends want to participate in this event as a team (up to 5 people), please let them know that they MUST put their team’s name during the registration process. Should you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to contact us at idrone.stem@gmail.com.

Happy building, coding, and flying!
