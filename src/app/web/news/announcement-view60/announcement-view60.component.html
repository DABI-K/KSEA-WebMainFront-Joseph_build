Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Call for Applications: 2023 Korea Technology Advisory Group (K-TAG)

August 25, 2023

Dear KSEA members,


KSEA would like to invite you to the "2023 Korea Technology Advisory Group"(K-TAG) organized by KIAT (Korea Institute for Advancement of Technology, 한국산업기술진흥원).
This invitation is a part of "International Collaborative Research and Development Program" initiated by MOTIE (산업통상자원부), and only K-TAG registered members will have the opportunity to participate in submitting the proposal, R&D consultants, Project Reviewer, etc.

Qualifications

  1. 1. Industries and Research Institutes in the U.S.
  • PhD degree in related field
  • Master's (Bachelor's) degree in related fields with 5+ years of experience (7 years for Bachelor's degree)

    2. Academies in the U.S.
  • Professor (Assistant Prof level or above) at accredited Universities/College or Community College

    3. U.S. Government
  • Grade 10 or above in government functions who has expertise in related fields

Areas of Expertise*

1. Machine/Materials 2. Electric/Electronic 3. Information/Communications 4. Chemistry 5. Bio/Medicine 6. Energy/Resource 7. Knowledge Service

(1.기계·소재 2.전기·전자 3.정보통신 4.화학 5.바이오·의료 6.에너지·자원 7.지식서비스)

- 7개 기술분과* 별로 모집하되 반도체, 배터리, 탄소중립, 디지털전환 등 한미 협력 유망 산업기술에 집중하여 선발

For details of the qualifications and expertise required, please refer to the attached guidelines.

If you would like to be in the K-TAG expert pool, please fill out the attached application form and send it to project@ksea.org by September 30, 2023

If you are already a member of K-TAG, you do NOT have to resubmit the application unless you need to update your information.

We look forward to seeing your active participation in this important R&D collaboration with KIAT.


Sincerely,


Yongho Sohn
President of KSEA
