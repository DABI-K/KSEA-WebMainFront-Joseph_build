Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Announcing Mid-Career Lunch & Networking at UKC 2023 - Reserve Your Spot Now!

July 5, 2023

Dear KSEA member,

We are delighted to announce the mid-career lunch & networking at UKC 2023, and we would like to extend an invitation for you to join us either on Thursday (August 3) or Friday (August 4). We have esteemed senior mentors at this semi-informal lunch program, including the three UKC 2023 keynote symposium chairs and two senior KSEA members with distinguished careers as follows:

Thursday, August 3

  • Prof. Youngsup Yoon (Emory University)
  • Prof. Young-Kee Kim (University of Chicago)
  • Prof. Jungsang Kim (Duke University)

Friday, August 4

  • Dr. Jae Hoon Kim (Boeing) - Mid-career professionals in the industry are encouraged to attend Friday lunch with Dr. Jae Hoon Kim.
  • Prof. Young-Shin Jun (Washington University in St. Louis)

Don't miss the opportunity to meet our esteemed senior mentors and enhance your professional networking connections with both mentors and your peers. We encourage you to register today! Seats are limited! This event operates on a first-come, first-served basis. The first 30 registrants for each day, with a valid KSEA User ID, will be accepted.

The PDF flyer, which includes a clickable registration link, is also attached to this email. Please feel free to share this email/flyer with your colleagues and friends, urging them to join us! Looking forward to meeting you all in Dallas this August at UKC 2023. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us (hyojin.kim.ksea@gmail.com).

Best regards,

Hyojin Kim, Associate Professor, New Jersey Institute of Technology
Brian Park, Professor, University of Virginia
Organizers, UKC 2023 Program for Mid-Career Professionals
