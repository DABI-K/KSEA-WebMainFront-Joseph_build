Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Call for Members of Korean-American AeroSpace Science and Technology Association (KASSTA)

February 07, 2024

Dear KSEA member,



We would like to invite you to join the Korean-American AeroSpace Science and Technology Association (KASSTA), which is being established for timely and broader collaborations in Space & Aeronautics related fields.
KASSTA is planned to be an Affiliated Professional Society of KSEA, when it is qualified and approved by the KSEA council.
Please provide your KSEA ID in the Google Form  to join KASSTA.
We will send you the Zoom link for the next KSSTA meeting once you complete the form.


Looking forward to hearing from you soon,
