Celebrating the life and career of KSEA 1st President Shoon Kyung Kim (Zoom: 8 pm EST, Tuesday, March 14)

March 2, 2023

Dear KSEA members, families and friends,

I am writing to invite you to the online event at 8 pm EST on Tuesday, March 14th to celebrate and remember the life and career of the KSEA 1st (1971-1972) President Shoon Kyung. At this event, we will look back on the past 50 years of the KSEA (its history and achievements) and discuss the future of KSEA in the light of the 20th anniversary of remembrance day of Dr. Shoon Kyung Kim.

Professor Shoon Kyung Kim (1920-2003) was Professor of Chemistry at Temple University (1969 - 1990), at Louisville University (1966 - 1969) and at Seoul National University (1951 - 1965). He was a representative scholar who achieved excellent academic performance in theoretical physics and chemistry. By organizing KSEA, he contributed to the development of science and technology and economic recovery of the homeland including support of young students studying abroad. As the founder of KSEA, Dr. Kim significantly tightened mutual relationships among Korean scientists working at science and engineering part in US and make a great contribution to development of homeland’s science and technology.

I hope to see many of you and your families and friends at this event on March 14th. English will be used during the entire zoom event.
