KSEA Distinguished Guest Series –GPT: Transforming Science, Engineering, and Beyond (Zoom: 8:30 pm ET, Wednesday, May 17)

May 1, 2023

Dear KSEA members, families, and friends,

I would like to invite you to “KSEA Distinguished Guest Series" that will be held on Wednesday, May 17 with the theme of “GPT: Transforming Science, Engineering, and Beyond”.

In this series, we will provide a gentle introduction to GPT, a cutting-edge AI text generation algorithm, and explore its impact on various fields such as science and engineering. We will discuss how it revolutionizes the way we work and delve into its broader social implications, shaping the future across multiple domains. The speaker is Kangwook Lee, Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Wisconsin, Madison. Prof. Lee leads deep learning research at Krafton and received the 2022 KSEA Young Investigator Grant.
