Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Additional Nomination for the 2023 Entrepreneur of the Year Award

April 4, 2023

Dear KSEA members and family,

The KSEA Honors and Awards committee decided to extend the nomination period for the Entrepreneur of the Year Award until April 15, 2023.


  • Entrepreneur of the Year Award to a member who has demonstrated stellar performance in the areas of entrepreneurship (Joint Award with Maeil Business Newspaper).

Please find the attached nomination form and submit it to sejong@ksea.org by April 15, 2023.

For any questions regarding the preparation of a nomination package, please contact the KSEA HQ at sejong@ksea.org or the HAC Chair, Dr. Hosin Lee at skytrust@gmail.com.

Thank you,

Hosin Lee, Ph.D.
KSEA Honors and Awards Committee Chair
