KSEA Senior Forum Series (Zoom: Thursday, April 27, 8 PM ET)

April 25, 2023

Dear KSEA members, families, and friends,

I am delighted to inform you that the next KSEA Senior Forum Series will take place at 8pm ET on Thursday, April 27. The 13th KSEA President Chan-Mo Park will present “Joyful and Thankful Life” (see more information from the attached poster), and the 28th KSEA President Hong Thomas Hahn will facilitate the event. Our goal is to provide KSEA seniors with a safe and supportive space to come together, learn from each other, and share experiences.

This monthly KSEA Senior Forum Series focuses on topics relevant to seniors, such as health and wellness, finances, sharing, social activities, education and self-care. Our forums are open to the public, and we invite KSEA seniors of all ages and backgrounds to join us.

Event Details:

  • Time: Thursday, April 27, 2023 at 5:00-7:00 pm (PT), 8:00-10:00pm (ET)
    [Click here to add it to Google Calendar]
  • Venue: Virtual. Anyone can join the meeting via Zoom [Link]
  • Facilitator: Thomas Hahn
  • Speaker: Chan-Mo Park “Joyful and Thankful Life”
  • Discussion: Hong Thomas Hahn
  • Questions: itm@ksea.org

I hope to see many of you and your families and friends at this meeting. You do not have to register for the meeting. I would greatly appreciate it if you could reach out to non-KSEA members to join or renew KSEA membership by using this [link]

Looking forward to seeing many of you at the zoom meeting on April 27th.
