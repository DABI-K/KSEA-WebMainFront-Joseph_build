KSEA Senior Forum Series (Zoom: March 30th, 8 PM ET / 5 PM PT)

April 25, 2023

Dear KSEA members, families, and friends,

I would like to invite you to the inaugural event of the “KSEA Senior Forum Series” that will be held on Thursday, March 30th via zoom online. The goal of this Series is to provide KSEA seniors with a safe and supportive space to come together, learn from each other, and share experiences.
We will be hosting monthly forums focusing on topics relevant to seniors, such as health and wellness, finances, and social activities. Our forums are open to the public, and we invite KSEA seniors of all ages and backgrounds, and their families and friends to join us.

Event Details:

  • Time: Thursday, March 30, 2023 at 5:00-7:00pm (PT), 8:00-10:00pm (ET)
  • Venue: Virtual. Anyone can join the meeting via Zoom [Link]
  • Host: President of KSEA Young-Kee Kim, University of Chicago
  • Speaker: Sung-Kwon Kang “Joy of Learning and Sharing
  • Discussion: Hong Thomas Hahn
  • Questions: itm@ksea.org

I hope to see many of you and your families and friends at this meeting. You do not have to register for the meeting.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could reach out to non-KSEA members to join or renew KSEA membership by using this [link]

Looking forward to seeing many of you at the zoom meeting on March 30th.

Sincerely,
Young-Kee Kim,
President of KSEA
