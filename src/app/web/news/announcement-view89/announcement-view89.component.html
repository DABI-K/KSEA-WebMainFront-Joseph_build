Announcement & Invitation - KSEA STEP-UP 2024


KSEA proudly announces its 5th Science and Technology Entrepreneurship Partners' Upscale Program (STEP-UP 2024) which will take place in Atlanta, GA on April 20~21, 2024!

Join us at STEP-UP 2024, where startups of various stages discover their full potential to become unicorns. Our theme this year is “Empowering Health and Technology Innovators: Bridging the Gap between Vision and Entrepreneurial Success” and we're excited to offer a range of dynamic programs designed to help technology startups achieve success.

Our Showcase of Success sessions will feature successful startups sharing their insider knowledge and know-hows of success. For those looking to take their startups to the next level, our Road to Success Sessions will cover critical topics such as marketing, business laws, intellectual properties (IP), and fundraising. You will also have the opportunity to pitch your current or future venture to potential investors and network with your future partners. Whether you're a budding entrepreneur or a seasoned founder, we have something for you at STEP-UP 2024. Don't miss out on this opportunity to learn, network, and grow your startup together!

- Date: April 20 ~ 21, 2024 (with an unofficial networking dinner on Friday, April 19)

- Venue: Omni Atlanta Hotel at Centennial Park @ 190 Marietta St NW, Atlanta, GA 30303

- Registration Dates:

  • Registration opens: March 14, 2024
  • Early Registration ($100 discount) ends: March 27, 2024
  • Registration closes: April 5, 2024 (or the maximum number of seats is reached.)

- Registration Fees:

  • Regular registration fee: $4004
  • $100 early registration discount (Register by March 27 and get $100 discount)
  • $100 KSEA member discount*
  • $100 KSEA undergraduate/graduate students and postdoc discount*
  • * Subject to membership verification. Visit https://www.ksea.org to check your membership status.

- Support for Attendees:
(Current KSEA membership and full attendance to the sessions are required)

  • Travel support: Up to $100 for airfare and driving mileage ($0.50/mile, minimum 100 miles)
  • Hotel accommodation: 2 nights for a double occupancy room (2 people/room)
    - Single occupancy is available at an additional $100 per night.
  • Hotel meals: 3 meals are provided (Saturday lunch and dinner, Sunday lunch)
  • $100 KSEA undergraduate/graduate students and postdoc discount*
  • Networking dinners are provided at discounted rates ($30 each) on Friday and Sunday

For details including the program and registration, please visit https://stepup.ksea.org/
For general inquiries, please contact us at stepup@ksea.org

We look forward to welcoming you at STEP-UP 2024 in Atlanta!
