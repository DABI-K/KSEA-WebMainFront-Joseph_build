KIC-Washington DC Internship Job Posting

Tech Commercialization Team Intern

About KIC:

Korea Innovation Center, Washington D.C., funded by the Korean government, aims to create a global startup ecosystem in Washington D.C., Silicon Valley, Berlin, and Beijing. We support and incubate Korean tech startups by providing connections, accelerators, and training programs. Over 100 Korean startups annually join us to explore the U.S. market, while we facilitate networking between leaders from various sectors in the U.S. and South Korea.
