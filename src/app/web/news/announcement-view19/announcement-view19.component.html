Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

The Women in STEM Workshop Series Hosted by KSEA in collaboration with KWiSE

February 23, 2023

Dear KSEA members and family,

I would like to invite you to “The Women in STEM Workshop Series” that will be held on Wednesday, March 8 with the theme of “Leadership and Allyship Towards Gender Equality In Celebration of International Women’s Day”.

International Women’s day is a day when women get an appreciation for all their hard-work and effort to maintain their lives and the lives of others around them. This workshop series is to honor women's significant achievements in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) careers, and place a much-needed focus on how to promote gender equity in education and work settings.

For the workshop hosted by KSEA and KWiSE, Dr. Seonmin Jin at Indiana University will present “Towards Gender Equality in the Workplace: Principles and Case Studies” followed by a panel discussion about Korean women in STEM.

Event Detail:

  • Time: Wednesday, March 8th, 2023 at 5:30-6:30pm (PT), 8:30-9:30pm (ET)
  • Theme: Leadership and Allyship Towards Gender Equality In Celebration of International Women’s Day
  • Venue: Virtual. Anyone can join the meeting via Zoom [Link]
  • Guests Speaker: Seonmi Jin, Indiana University
  • Host: President of KSEA Young-Kee Kim, University of Chicago
  • Questions: itm@ksea.org

I hope to see many of you and your families and friends at this meeting. You do not have to register for the meeting.

I would greatly appreciate it if you could reach out to non-KSEA members to join or renew KSEA membership by using this [link]

Look forward to seeing many of you at the zoom meeting on March 8th.

Sincerely,
