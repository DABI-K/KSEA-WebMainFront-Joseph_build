Dear KSEA

KSEA is pleased to host the 2nd Mid-Career Development Workshop. We are honored to have three distinguished Korean-American leaders in academia as panelists. This event is free to KSEA active members (please join/renew your membership https://www.ksea.org/signUp and complete purchasing a membership). We will have up to 45 participants (first come, first serve).

Event Detail:

Our panelists are Prof. Young-Shin Jun (Washington University in St. Lous), Prof. Nosang Myung (University of Notre Dame), and Prof. Young-sup Yoon (Emory University). Prof. Byungkyu Brian Park (University of Virginia) will moderate the workshop.

  • At 8 PM ET, Thurs., February 23, 2023
  • 8 - 9 PM: Presentations by Panelists (about 15 min. each)
  • 9 - 10 PM: Breakout sessions
  • Virtual Zoom (zoom link will be sent to registered participants)

This workshop is designed for those who need a refresh on their academic careers, primarily through sharing personal experiences by the panel and the opportunity to build peer networking among participants. KSEA plans to have a gathering at the UKC 2023 in Dallas. If you are no longer a junior faculty but seeking a full professorship and have experienced any mid-career crisis, this workshop is for you!

Raffle: After the event, we will select two participants by drawing an online raffle and send them Amazon gift cards as a token of gratitude ($25 each).
