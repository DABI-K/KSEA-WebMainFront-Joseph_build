Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Korea-US Advanced Industry and Technology Cooperation Forum 2023

September 11, 2023

Dear KSEA members,


The Ministry of Technology, Industry and Energy, along with the Korea Institute for Advancement of Technology, is pleased to announce the upcoming "Korea-US Advanced Industry and Technology Cooperation Forum 2023," scheduled to be held in Seoul on September 21st.

Building on the success of last year's event in Washington, D.C., this year's Forum represents its second iteration. The Ministry of Technology, Industry and Energy, along with the Korea Institute for Advancement of Technology are committed to further solidifying the partnership between our two nations. This includes fostering dynamic collaboration in advanced industrial technology between our small and medium-sized enterprises and leading innovation institutions in the United States. The Forum also aims to inject renewed vigor into the research landscape.

Under the theme "Securing Prosperous Future: Shared Values and Sustainable Partnership," representatives from both governments, academia, industry, research, and governmental sectors will gather to deliberate on advanced industry development and technological innovation. The Forum will be an invaluable platform for sharing future visions and exploring avenues for technological collaboration.

We cordially invite you to participate in the "Korea-US Advanced Industry and Technology Cooperation Forum 2023."

For more information and registration, please visit: https://www.k-itcforum.or.kr/
Registration is available for in-person and virtual participation.
If you have any questions, please contact KIAT US office at kiat.usoffice@gmail.com
