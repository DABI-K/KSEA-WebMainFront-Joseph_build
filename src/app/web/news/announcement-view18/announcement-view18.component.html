Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

KSEA Python Instructional Series on Thursdays, March 9th, 16th, and 23rd, 2023

February 17, 2023

Dear KSEA members and family,

The last 4th KSEA Distinguished Guest Series by Professors Daniel Lee and SueYeon Chung on AI for Science, Technology, and Society was a success and attended by over 100 audience members. We would like to extend the theme of AI by showing you the basics and AI applications of Python, the most popular programming language used in data science and machine learning. I would like to invite you to the KSEA Python Instructional Series to be held on 3 consecutive Thursdays next month in March. No prior Python programming experience is necessary. You just need a computer with a web browser. Our guest Python instructors are June Park, MS, from Daugherty Business Systems, David Ho, Ph.D., from Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, and Benjamin Lee, Ph.D., from Weill Cornell Medicine.

Event Detail:

Time: Thursdays, March 9th, 16th, and 23rd, 2023 at 5:30-7:00 PM (PT), 8:30-10:00 PM (ET)

Theme: Python Instructional Series

  • March 9th - “Python for Absolute Beginners” - Learn how to manipulate python lists and a guide to self-study
  • March 16th - “Python for Data Science” - Learn Pandas to analyze spreadsheet data and visualize them
  • March 23rd - “Python for AI Applications” - Play with deep learning AI models using images and other big data

Venue: Virtual. Anyone can register and a zoom link with Python setup instructions will be sent on Sunday, March 5, 2023 to KSEA members who register by filling out this Google form (HERE)

Registration Deadline: Friday, March 3, 2023

Cost: $0 (discounted)

Host: President of KSEA Young-Kee Kim (University of Chicago)

Questions: itm@ksea.org

I hope to see many of you and interested family members join these sessions. I would greatly appreciate it if you could reach out to non-KSEA members to join or renew their KSEA membership by using this sign-up link (HERE).

Instructors: June Park (Data Engineer), David Ho (Machine Learning Scientist), Benjamin Lee (Senior Research Associate in Radiology)

Look forward to seeing many of you at the first online meeting on March 9th.

Sincerely,
Young-Kee Kim
President of KSEA
