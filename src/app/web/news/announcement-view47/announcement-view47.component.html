Announcement

Korean-American Scientists and Engineers Association (KSEA)

Call for the 2023 KSEA Nominations

May 31, 2023

Dear KSEA members,

It is again the time for nominations of various KSEA positions including the President, Vice Presidents, Auditors, Technical Group Councilors, and Committee members.

Detailed openings can be found from the attached vacancy list.

You can nominate others and/or yourself. Nominees must be a KSEA member who has paid the 51st KSEA (2022-2023) membership due at the time of nomination.

Please fill out the attached nomination form for each position and forward it to nomination@ksea.org as soon as possible but no later than July 7, 2023. The nomination form should NOT exceed 1 page with a minimum font size of 11.

The nomination committee will submit the list of recommended candidates to the President and the Council as specified in the KSEA Policy.

Thank you for your support.

Byungkyu Brian Park
Nomination Committee

Young-Kee Kim
President of KSEA
