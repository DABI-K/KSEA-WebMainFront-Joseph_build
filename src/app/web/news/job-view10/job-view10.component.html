Recommencement of Open Invitations for the 9th President of GIST

January 20, 2023

Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST, www.gist.ac.kr) is a Korean government-funded, research-oriented institute founded in 1993 to educate the next-generation of researchers in advanced science and technology in accordance with the Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology Act.
Ahead of the 30th anniversary of its establishment, GIST has become one of the most distinguished universities in Korea with its globally recognized education and research capabilities in a short time.
GIST College, which was established in 2010, emphasizes innovative education to prepare students to become convergence specialists by endowing them with advanced scientific expertise that is reinforced with a broad background in the humanities.
GIST now invites all qualified persons to apply for the position of President of GIST to lead the future of GIST on a global scale with vision, integrity, and leadership.

Requirements of Applicants

  • Be able to carry out all official duties of and on behalf of GIST
  • Be able to supervise the faculty and staff of GIST with exemplary professionalism
  • Be able to develop GIST as a world-renowned research institute through superb administrative and managerial abilities
  • Must have and maintain high standards of ethics, integrity and morality
  • Must not fall under the purview of Article 19 of the GIST Articles of Association (see below)

Required Documents

  • One copy of Application Form and Resume (prescribed form)
  • One copy of Pre-Questionnaire for Applicants (prescribed form)
  • One copy of Key Achievements and Description (less than 5pages, A4 size)
  • One copy of Detailed Plan of Institutional Development and Management (less than 5pages, A4 size)
  • Signed copy of Privacy Consent Agreement for Information Collection and Usage (prescribed form)
