Programs

Chapter Support

Your donation will be allocated to our local chapters to support their activities. If you would like to specify a particular chapter to receive your contribution, please get in touch with our headquarter at finance@ksea.org.

Education Support

Education is at the heart of KSEA’s values. Your contribution will help to fund our educational initiatives, including the National Math & Science Competition, scholarships and awards, the Distinguished Guest Series, workshops, and more.

Next Generation Support

Our Next (Young) Generation community is constantly expanding with new initiatives to connect and support one another. Your contribution will help to foster the growth and development of our future generations.

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Support

KSEA is committed to supporting our members in their efforts to commercialize technology by promoting entrepreneurship. Your support will help us to continue our work in educating and incubating the next generation of entrepreneurs.

Mid-Career Member Support

KSEA offers programs to support its mid-career professionals for their career development in both academia and industry. This growing population requires significant resources and guidance from our experienced members. You can help strengthen KSEA’s support for its mid-career members.

Senior Member Support

KSEA offers programs to support its senior members in their continued professional growth and retirement life. Our experienced members have a wealth of knowledge and expertise to share, and your contribution can help us provide them with the resources they need to continue making an impact in their fields.

General Support

By donating to KSEA’s areas of greatest need, you enable the Association to bolster its most important priorities and confidently tackle new challenges and opportunities.
